## 基本信息

- **标题**: Translation Asymmetry in LLMs as a Data Augmentation Factor: A Case Study for 6 Romansh Language Varieties
- **作者**: Jannis Vamvas, Ignacio Pérez Prat, Angela Heldstab, Dominic P. Fischer, Sina Ahmadi et al.
- **ArXiv ID**: 2603.25489v1
- **发布日期**: 2026-03-26
- **主分类**: cs.CL
- **分类**: cs.CL
- **摘要**: Recent strategies for low-resource machine translation rely on LLMs to generate synthetic data from higher-resource languages. We find that this method fails for Romansh, because LLMs tend to confuse its 6 distinct language varieties. Our experiments show that instead, the direction of data augmentation should be aligned with the resource gradient between source and target language. This approach surpasses Gemini 3 Pro in the lowest-resource variety of Romansh by 23 BLEU. A human evaluation confirms that our experiments yield the first model that generates fluent translations in the individual Romansh varieties.
